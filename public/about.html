Why We Started

As college students ourselves, we saw the problem first-hand: new laptops cost a semester's worth of fees, yet millions of perfectly functional devices sat unused, rapidly losing value in people's homes.

We created Retech Revival to solve both problems simultaneously — giving buyers access to affordable, certified devices and giving sellers a fair, transparent price for their old tech.

Every device we refurbish is one less piece of e-waste. Every student who buys from us saves money they can invest in their education.
